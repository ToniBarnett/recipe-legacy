Welcome to Recipe Legacy!

Preserving recipes · Sharing traditions

Where every recipe tells a story...

Recipes connect generations. They remind us of the people, meals, and memories that bring families together. Recipe Legacy is a place where family recipes can be viewed today and preserved for tomorrow.

Whether it is a favorite holiday dessert, a comforting family meal, or a recipe passed down through genrations, each one tells part of a family's story.

This website is the first version of Recipe Legacy and was created as a class project. It currently includes three recipes from three different categories to demonstrate the dsign and organziation of the site. As the project grows, more family recipes and features will be added.

My long-term vision is to create a website where approved family members can help grow the collection by adding and updating recipes. Instead of deleting recipes, older or unused recipes can be archived so they remain part of our family's history. Future versions will also preserve the sotries and memories that make each recipe special
