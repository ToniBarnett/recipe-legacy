Aunt Evelyn's Punch

Category: Beverage

Ingredients

  • 1 quart Dole Pineapple juice
  • 1 cup Tropicana Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Real Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 cup Real Lime Juice
  • 1 cup Domino Sugar
  • 2 liters Canada Dry Ginger Ale
  • 1/2 liter Canada Dry Club Soda

Directions

  1. Combine pineapple juice, orange juice, lemon juice, lime juice, and sugar in a large pitcher.
  2. Stir until the sugar is completely dissolved.
  3. Add ginger ale and club soda.
  4. Stir gently until combined to preserve the carbonation.
  5. Serve over ice and enjoy.

Golden Grilled Cheese

Category: Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of sandwich bread
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • 2 slices of sharp cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Spread butter evenly on each side of each slice of bread.
  2. Sprinkle a little garlic powder and pepper on the buttered sides.
  3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the cheddar cheese.
  5. Top with second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  6. Cook 2-3 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Flip and cook another 2-3 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is completely melted.
  8. Serve hot with Seasoned Tomato Soup.

Seasoned Tomato Soup

Category: Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.75 oz) can condensed tomato soup
  • 1 soup can of milk (use the empty soup to measure)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon dried basil or parsley for garnish

Directions

  1. Open the 10.75 oz can of condensed tomato soup.
  2. Empty the can into the saucepan.
  3. Fill that same empty soup can with regular milk and add it to the saucepan.
  4. Stir in the milk until smooth.
  5. Add the butter, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper.
  6. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the butter is melted and the soup is hot.
  7. Do not let the soup boil.
  8. Garnish with dried basil or parsley if desired and serve warm.
  9. Serve with Golden Grilled Cheese.
